6th generation (1995-2000)

Bigger than the previous generation, the 6th-generation hatchback – launched in September 1995 – had the same 262 cm wheelbase as coupes and saloons. No longer a compact supermini, it was a step up into family hatchback territory. It was a leap forward in in-car entertainment, too, and this generation was the first to come with a CD player. In 1997, Honda launched the high-performance Civic Type R – the R stood for racing.
